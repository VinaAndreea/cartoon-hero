Spaghetti alla Puttanesca

Ingredients

  1. 3 tbsp olive oil
  2. 2 large garlic cloves, crushed
  3. ½ tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  4. 400g can chopped tomatoes
  5. 5 anchovy fillets, finely chopped
  6. 120g pitted black olives
  7. 2 tbsp capers, drained
  8. 300g dried spaghetti
  9. ½ small bunch of parsley, finely chopped

Method

      Heat the oil in a non-stick pan over a medium-low heat. Add the onion along with a generous pinch of salt and fry for 10 mins, or until soft. Add the garlic and chilli, if using, and cook for a further minute.

      Stir the tomatoes, anchovies, olives and capers into the onion, bring to a gentle simmer and cook, uncovered, for 15 mins. Season to taste.
